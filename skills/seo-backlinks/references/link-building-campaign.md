# Link Building Campaign Framework

Active link-acquisition playbook. Use this alongside the audit framework in `SKILL.md` -- audit tells you where you stand; this guides where to go next.

## Campaign Types

| Type | Best For | Target DA | Success Rate | Timeline/Placement |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Guest Post Outreach** | Authority, brand awareness | 40+ | 10-15% | 2-4 weeks |
| **Resource Page Link** | Quick wins, educational content | 30+ | 15-20% | 1-2 weeks |
| **Broken Link Building** | Replacing dead competitor links | 35+ | 20-30% | 2-3 weeks |
| **HARO / Source Requests** | High-authority press links | 60+ | 5-10% | Ongoing daily |
| **Digital PR** | Brand mentions, top-tier links | 70+ | 5-8% | 4-8 weeks |
| **Niche Directory Submissions** | Local SEO, niche authority | 25+ | 80-90% | 1 week |

### Process Summary

**Guest Post:** identify blogs → analyze content quality → pitch topic ideas → write 1500+ word piece → contextual link (1-2 per post).

**Resource Page:** search `intitle:resources + keyword` → create valuable resource (tool, guide, infographic) → pitch addition → highlight unique value.

**Broken Link:** scan target sites with Check My Links → create replacement content → notify owner of 404 → suggest your content.

**HARO / source requests:** subscribe to query feeds (HARO, Qwoted, SourceBottle, etc., 3× daily) → respond within 2 hours → expert quotes with credentials → follow up if featured.

**Digital PR:** create newsworthy content (data study, survey, report) → build journalist outreach list → press release + pitch → strategic follow-up.

**Directories:** identify quality directories (not spam farms) → submit accurate business info → track status → monitor approval. See `directory-submissions.md`.

## Target Selection Criteria

### Domain Metrics

- **Minimum DA/DR**: 30 (adjust by campaign type)
- **Organic Traffic**: 5k+/month
- **Spam Score**: <30%
- **Active Content**: Published within 30 days

### Relevance Criteria

- **Topical Alignment**: Same/adjacent niche
- **Audience Overlap**: Target demographics match
- **Content Quality**: Well-written, valuable
- **Engagement**: Comments, shares, backlinks

### Link Opportunity Signals

- "Write for us" page exists
- Resource/links page exists
- Competitor backlinks present
- Active blog with guest authors
- Contact info easily accessible

## Outreach Workflow

| Phase | Week | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Research** | 1 | Identify 100 target prospects; score by DA, relevance, opportunity; export to outreach sheet; find decision-maker contact info |
| **2. Initial Outreach** | 2 | Send personalized emails (20-30/day); A/B test subject lines; track open/response rates; log all communications |
| **3. Follow-Up** | 3-4 | First follow-up 3 days after initial; second 7 days after first; final 14 days after second; mark unresponsive as "dead" |
| **4. Execution** | 5-8 | Fulfill content requirements; submit for review; make revisions; confirm publication + link |
| **5. Tracking** | Ongoing | Monitor link status monthly; track referral traffic; measure ranking impact; build relationship for future opportunities |

## Follow-Up Sequences

**Guest Post:**
- Email 1 (Day 0): Personalized pitch with topic ideas
- Email 2 (Day 3): "Just checking in" + additional value
- Email 3 (Day 10): Alternative angle/topic offer
- Email 4 (Day 21): Final attempt with social proof

**Resource Link:**
- Email 1 (Day 0): Notify of resource + value prop
- Email 2 (Day 5): Brief reminder with quick link
- Email 3 (Day 14): Final gentle nudge

**Broken Link:**
- Email 1 (Day 0): Helpful broken link notification
- Email 2 (Day 4): Offer replacement content
- Email 3 (Day 12): Last follow-up

> Email templates for each phase live in `outreach-templates.md`.

## Success Metrics

### Campaign KPIs

- **Response Rate**: target 30%+
- **Conversion Rate** (link secured): target 10%+
- **Average DA**: target 40+
- **DoFollow Ratio**: target 70%+
- **Time to Link**: target <30 days

### ROI Metrics

- **Cost per Link**: track time/money invested
- **Link Value**: DA × Relevance × Placement score
- **Ranking Impact**: target keyword position changes
- **Referral Traffic**: clicks from acquired links
- **Domain Authority Growth**: monthly DR/DA tracking

### Quality Metrics

- **Link Retention**: >95% after 6 months
- **Toxic Score**: <5% of acquired links
- **Editorial Ratio**: 80%+ editorial (not footer/sidebar)
- **Anchor Text Diversity**: <10% exact match

## Optimization Levers

### A/B Test Elements

- Subject lines (test 2-3 variants)
- Email length (short vs detailed)
- Call-to-action wording
- Personalization depth
- Send day/time

### Performance Analysis

- Track response rates by template
- Identify high-converting topics
- Monitor DA correlation with success
- Analyze follow-up effectiveness
- Measure seasonal trends

### Continuous Improvement

- Update templates based on data
- Refine targeting criteria
- Adjust outreach volume
- Optimize follow-up timing
- Build relationship database
